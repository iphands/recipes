# Ingredients
- 2 stick butter
- 8 large onions
- 3 stems celery
- 6 cloves garlic
- 3 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
- 2 teaspoons prepared mustard
- 1/4 cup worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 3 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon celery seed
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- red pepper

# Directions
1. Melt 2 sticks butter
2. Saute:
 - onions
 - bell pepper
 - celery
 - garlic cloves
3. Add to the saute
 - sweet pickle relish
 - prepared mustard
 - w. sauce ??
 - vinegar
 - salt
 - white pepper
 - celery seed
 - brown sugar
 - red pepper to taste
4. Simmer until reduced slightly
