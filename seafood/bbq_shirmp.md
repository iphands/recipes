# Ingredients
- 2 lbs shrimp with shells
- 2 sticks of butter
- Dash of wine vinegar
- Worcestershire sauce
- Tabasco

# Things
- Pit
- Foil

# Directions
1. Overnight marinate shrimp in
   - Melted butter
   - Wine vinegar
   - Tabasco
   - Worcestershire sauce
2. Cook on pit (foiled) until pink
