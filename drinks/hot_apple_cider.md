# Ingredients
- 24 whole cloves
- 24 whole allspice
- 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
- 2 cinnamon sticks
- 2 oranges
- 1 gallon apple cider

# Things
- Crock pot

# Directions
1. Squeeze juice of oranges in a crock pot
1. Add the rest and heat on warm
1. Cook for ~1 hour
1. Top with slices of orange
