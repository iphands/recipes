# Ingredients
## Chaffle (makes two mini)
- 1/2 cup sharp warm motzeralla cheese
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp almond flour
## Fillins
- 4 slices Summer sausage
- 2 slices bologna
- 1 egg

# Directions
## Chaffle
1. Mix all
1. Oil waffle iron
1. Cook until crisp
## Fillins
1. Fry egg
1. Brown meats
1. Assemble
