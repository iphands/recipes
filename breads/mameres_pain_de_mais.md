# Ingredients

- 2 cups cornmeal
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 5 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 oil
- 1 beaten egg
- 2 cups of milk

# Directions
1. Mix
   - cornmeal
   - flour
   - salt
   - sugar
   - baking powder
2. In a measuring cup mix
   - oil
   - beaten egg
   - fill with milk to the 2 cup line
3. Add liquid to dry mix
4. Grease skillet
5. Bake at 350F for 40 minutes
